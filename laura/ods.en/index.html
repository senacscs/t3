SDG 4 Explanation

O ODS 4 tends to talk and explain about the need to "ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all". In other words, SDG 4 intends to offer people a quality education, with the objective that everyone can complete primary and higher education so that in the future they can guarantee that all young people and adults are literate and that they are inside the basic knowledge in reading, writing and mathematics has context menu.

Goals

Quality Education



ABy 2030, ensure that all girls and boys complete primary and secondary education, which is equitable and of good quality, at the appropriate age, ensuring that it is offered free of charge in the public network and that it leads to satisfactory and relevant learning outcomes.





By 2030, ensure that all young people and adults are literate, having acquired basic knowledge in reading, writing and mathematics.
