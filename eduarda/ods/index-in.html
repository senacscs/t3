SDG 3

Good Health and Well-Being

Author Analysis

SDG 3 (Health and Well-being) has as its main goal to bring physical and mental health, as well as a high quality lifestyle, to everyone, spanning all age groups. Some of the targets of SDG 3 include disease prevention and treatment, reduction of maternal and child mortality, combating epidemics, strengthening the responsiveness of healthcare systems, and promoting access to safe and effective medicines, while also fostering research and vaccine development. The Institute of Applied Economic Research (IPEA) provides various indicators that monitor the progress of this sustainable development goal in Brazil. The most notable ones include infant mortality rate, life expectancy at birth, and vaccination coverage. Overall, these indicators ensure access to necessary care for the survival of children, measure the quality of healthcare systems and lifestyles, and the proportion of immunized individuals. Thus, they provide data that contribute to health and well-being. The IBGE Census also provides contributing data, mainly of demographic nature. Through it, we can identify inequalities in relation to SDG 3. The Census can highlight disparities in access to healthcare services and other social determinants. Identifying these inequalities is as crucial as identifying progress, as from problems, we can derive solutions.

Practical Actions and Goals

3.4-By 2030, reduce by one third premature mortality from non-communicable diseases through prevention and treatment and promote mental health and well-being.


By 2020, halve the number of global deaths and injuries from road traffic accidents.


PRACTICAL ACTIONS:

1- Implement a program for the early diagnosis of non-communicable diseases, also guaranteeing access to medicines
2- Develop programs in schools that make people aware of the risks of non-communicable diseases and the importance of leading a healthy lifestyle;
3- Implement technology in health, creating an application where patients can self-manage their health, and also to provide supporting information;
4- Criar leis de trânsito mais rígidas;
5- Invest is resources for the maintenance and construction of safer road;
6- Promover programas de educação para segurança no trânsito em escolas, comunidades e locais de trabalho, visando aumentar a conscientização sobre comportamentos seguros, respeito às regras de trânsito e uso adequado de dispositivos de segurança.
