SDG 13 - Climate Action

Davi R. Borba
Guilherme C. Müller

What are SDGs?

Sustainable Development Goal 13 (SDG 13) is to limit and adapt to climate change. It is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals established by the United Nations General Assembly in 2015. The official mission of this goal is “Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts”. SDG 13 aims to promote actions on a global and local scale to mitigate the effects of climate change and build resilience in the face of already existing impacts. In addition, he emphasizes the importance of making governments, companies, organizations and the population in general aware of the need to adopt sustainable practices, aiming to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and improve energy efficiency.

About SDG 13:

Goals

13.1 : Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related risks and natural disasters in all countries

13.2 : Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies and planning

13.3 : Improve education, raise awareness and human and institutional capacity on mitigation, adaptation, impact reduction and early warning of climate change

Communities can create specific action plans to address the impacts of climate change that affect their regions. This includes identifying vulnerable areas, investing in resilient infrastructure, improving water resource management and promoting sustainable agricultural practices
Encouraging the use of public transport and creating infrastructure for cyclists can significantly reduce the greenhouse gas emissions associated with transport. In addition, educational campaigns can make people aware of the environmental impacts of excessive use of private vehicles and promote more sustainable travel habits.
Schools and communities can create environmental education programs that address issues related to climate change, its causes and consequences, as well as practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint and promote sustainability. This can include classes, workshops, lectures and hands-on activities.
